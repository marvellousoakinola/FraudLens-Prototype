FraudLens was conceived and developed as a complete cybersecurity tool focused on making threat detection accessible and actionable. The platform was built from the ground up with a strong emphasis on both technical accuracy and user experience.

The entire frontend was developed using **React and TypeScript**, including the scanning interface, results analysis, authentication flow, and dashboard. Significant effort was also invested in integrating with backend APIs, debugging system stability issues, and ensuring the application functions as a cohesive product.
